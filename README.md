Profit Analysis Dashboard — Power BI Project

It analyzes company performance across three U.S. states — California, Florida, and New York — and provides key financial insights based on:
-> Profit

-> R&D Spend

-> Marketing Spend

-> Administration Spend

Insights :

1. Overall Profit Performance

-> Total Profit: 5.60M

-> Average Profit per Record: 112K

-> Max Profit: 192K

-> Min Profit: 15K

2. Profit by State

 ->New York generates the highest profit (1.93M)

-> Florida follows with 1.90M

-> California generates 1.77M

➡️ New York is the top-performing state in terms of profit.

3. Category-Wise Expenditure Impact

-> Marketing Spend is the highest contributor among expenditures: 10.55M

-> Administration Spend: 6.07M

-> R&D Spend: 3.69M

➡️ Marketing receives the highest investment, indicating strong focus on promotional activities.

 4. R&D Spend vs Profit

-> States with higher R&D investment tend to display higher profit, especially New York.

➡️ R&D positively correlates with profit.

 5. Administration Spend vs Profit

-> Administration expenses remain consistent across all states, but profits vary.

➡️ Administration cost has lesser direct impact on profit.

 6. Marketing Spend vs Profit

-> Florida spends the most on marketing (~4.0M) and shows strong profit numbers.

-> New York and California have comparatively lower marketing spend but still produce notable profits.

➡️ Marketing spend influences profit but is not the only driving factor.

 7. Key Conclusions

-> New York is the most profitable state.

->Marketing Spend is the largest cost category.

-> R&D has a stronger correlation with profit compared to administration or marketing.

-> Balanced spending across R&D and Marketing appears to produce the best results.
